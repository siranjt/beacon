The Fixed Point

A fixed point that doesn't move when the world does.

The harbor is old. The stones are weathered. The storm tonight is the same storm as a hundred years before — and the lighthouse has stood through every one of them.

Beacon is the platform. Six umbrella agents woven into one. Customer · Performance · Escalation · Post-Payment · Miss-Payment · Negative Keyword. One launcher. One sign-in. One ground that doesn't shift.

II.
01SOURCE 02FLICKER 03BEARING
The Source

Small. Patient. Watchful.

Atop the beacon burns a flame. It moves with the wind. It bends. It flickers. It does not go out.

The flame is what makes a beacon a beacon. Without it, there is only stone — a tall building on a cliff, telling no one anything.

The flame doesn't speak. It doesn't decide. It is the source — the thing that turns weather into warning, dark into bearing.

III.
01SWEEP 02REVEAL 03MOVE ON
The Reveal

It does not decide. It does not remember. It reveals.

From the flame comes the beam — a sweeping cone of warm light, cutting across the rain, falling on the rocks, the sea, the cliffs. Illuminating, for one moment, what the dark was hiding. Then it moves on.

Ask Beam a question, and it shows you what it sees, the moment you ask. Clean, fast, cited.

IV.
01CLOAK 02BLADE 03WATCH
The One Who Stands Watch

A beam without a Keeper is just light pointing nowhere.

Pan down. At the base of the lighthouse — half in shadow, half in the warm glow of the flame above — a figure. A long cloak, fur-lined against the rain. A sword at the hip. The hood drawn against the wind.

The Keeper is not the beacon. Not the flame. Not the beam. The Keeper is the one who safeguards them all.

Without the Keeper, the flame would die in the first storm. The beam would forget what it last revealed. The story would be lost between sweeps.

Three Waves That Built the Watch

— Wave I

How the Keeper hears

pgvector cosine (top 50) and tsvector match (top 50) run in parallel, fused by RRF (k=60), reranked by Voyage rerank-2.5-lite. The right 5 of ~40 candidates land in Beam's context. Hybrid catches what either alone misses.

— Wave II

How the Keeper decides

When two facts collide, deterministic ranking picks the winner using the trust hierarchy. Losers stay in the audit log via superseded_by. Beam only sees the winner by default. No LLM in the loop. Auditable.

— Wave III

How the watch stays fast

Heavy context loaders cache their blobs for 5 minutes. Stage A refresh busts the cache the moment new data lands. AMs feel the snap on every follow-up question.

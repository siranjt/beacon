The trust layer

Every answer cites. Every fact is sourced.

Beam tells you where it got every claim. Keeper preserves the AM-spoken quote behind every Haiku-extracted fact. Nothing is final, nothing is lost.

Inline citations

Every claim Beam makes carries a cite chip. Click it to see the source fact, the AM-spoken quote that grounded it, and when it was last refined. No more "Beam said it, so it must be true" — you can always verify.

Calibrated confidence

Beam reports High / Medium / Low confidence based on source trust, recency, and corroboration. A fresh BaseSheet fact = High. A Beam-conversation inference with no Keeper backing = Low. Calibration tracked against AM thumbs.

Full version log

Every Keeper write — create, refine, confirm, reject, supersede — appends to beacon_brain_fact_versions. Soft-delete only. Nothing is ever truly gone.

Validate inbox

Haiku-extracted candidates land in a triage queue. AMs confirm, edit, reject, or reclassify. Source quotes shown alongside every candidate so the AM sees exactly what the model saw.

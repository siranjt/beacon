Fresh. Warming. Resurrected.

Spot the resurrection in real time.

A customer who churned and resubbed gets a "Resurrected · 3 days" chip. Don't penalize them for empty signals — they're new in the book again.

Fresh

Joined under 14 days ago. No score yet — signals still warming.

0–14 days

Warming

Some signals landing. Score is provisional — interpret it lightly.

14–45 days

Active

Full signal coverage. Score is reliable. Standard triage rules apply.

45+ days

Resurrected

Returned after a churn or pause. Treat as fresh until comms re-stabilize.

Returned < 30d

Lifecycle tags appear above the composite score on the card. As a manager, watch for sudden spikes in Resurrected — it's a win signal.

For new books

Fresh + Warming look low

An AM with a high count of fresh/warming customers will look worse on raw red %. Filter them out when assessing.

For win-backs

Resurrected = team win

Every "Resurrected" chip is someone who chose to come back. Celebrate them publicly — and study what changed.

For accuracy

Active is when the score is real

Only customers at 45+ days have signal coverage strong enough to be treated as definitive. Hold judgment before that.
